Homeowner protection

Before hiring, verify the provider like the work depends on it.

Concrete Service helps with matching, but the final hiring decision belongs to the homeowner. The strongest aggregator experience is honest about that boundary.

Ask every provider for:

Current license information where required by your location and project type.
Insurance details before work begins on your property.
A written estimate with scope, prep, materials, thickness, finish, timeline, and payment terms.
Clear warranty, cleanup, curing, and access expectations in writing.
