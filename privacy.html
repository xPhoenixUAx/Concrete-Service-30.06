Privacy Policy

This policy explains how Concrete Service may collect and use information submitted through this free provider matching website.

Information We Collect

We may collect contact details, project descriptions, location information, service preferences, and communication records when you submit a request or contact us.

How Information Is Used

Information may be used to respond to requests, help connect you with independent local providers, improve the website, prevent misuse, and communicate about your inquiry.

Sharing With Providers

When you request a match, relevant information may be shared with independent providers so they can contact you, review the project, and provide estimates. Providers are independent businesses and are responsible for their own privacy practices.

Your Choices

You may contact us at to request updates or deletion where applicable. We may retain limited records for legal, operational, or anti-fraud purposes.
